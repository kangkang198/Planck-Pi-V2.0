解决播放声音变快的问题

修改drivers/clk/sunxi-ng/ccu-suniv-f1c100s.c 中 311行

static SUNXI_CCU_GATE(codec_clk, "codec", "pll-audio", 0x140, BIT(31), 0);
改为
static SUNXI_CCU_GATE(codec_clk, "codec", "pll-audio", 0x140, BIT(31), CLK_SET_RATE_PARENT);
